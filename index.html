VA-π: Variational Policy Alignment for Pixel-Aware Autoregressive Generation

Xinyao Liao*1 Qiyuan He*†2 Kai Xu2 Xiaoye Qu1 Yicong Li2 Wei Wei1 Angela Yao2
1Huazhong University of Science and Technology 2National University of Singapore
arXiv (coming soon) Code

TL;DR: How should visual autoregressive models be optimized? Token space or pixel space? We argue the answer is pixel space. We propose VA-π, a lightweight post-training framework that directly optimizes visual AR models with a principled pixel-space objective, solved by introducing evidence lower bound (ELBO) that unifies pixel reconstruction and autoregressive modeling. To optimize under the discrete token space, VA-π introduces a reinforcement-based alignment strategy that treats the AR generator as a policy, uses pixel-space reconstruction quality as its intrinsic reward and Next Token Prediction (NTP) loss with noisy context as regulizer.

Variational alignment objective
An ELBO-style objective that unifies pixel reconstruction and autoregressive modeling.
Pixel-aware intrinsic reward
Use pixel-space reconstruction quality as intrinsic feedback for discrete token sequences.
Lightweight post-training
No tokenizer retraining and no external reward model; keep the AR distribution stable with NTP regularization.
